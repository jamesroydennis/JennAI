Logo Prompt for JennAI:

"Minimalist and modern tech logo for 'JennAI', an AI software assistant.

Design Elements: Consider a stylized letter 'J' subtly integrated or intertwined with abstract neural network patterns, subtle circuit lines, or a clean, abstract representation of a thought process or data flow. The 'AI' part could be represented through these tech elements or a modern, clean typeface.

Style: Clean vector art, sleek, professional, and innovative. The logo should feel intelligent and approachable, not overly complex or intimidating.

Color Palette: Primary colors could be deep blues or teals, accented with silver, white, or a subtle vibrant color (like a light electric blue or a soft purple) for a touch of dynamism. Avoid overly playful or cartoonish colors.

Overall Feel: The logo should be suitable for a sophisticated software project, conveying trust, intelligence, and cutting-edge technology."
